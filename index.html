About Me

My name is Laurel and I am a graduate student in the physics program at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I do observational astronomy research on galaxy clusters with Professor Michael McDonald.

I am originally from Syracuse, NY and I have a B.S. in Physics with a minor in Mathematics from Syracuse University. During my four years there, I conducted gravitational-wave research with the Laser Interferometer Gravitational-Wave Observatory and the Syracuse University Gravitational Wave Research Group under Professors Peter Saulson and Duncan Brown.
